How We Work

Our Culture

Three principles that define how we operate, build, and grow every single day.

Truth Over Comfort

We tell users what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. Honest financial guidance, even when it's difficult, produces better long-term outcomes. We hold that same standard internally — honest feedback, data-driven decisions, no sacred cows.

Mission-First Hiring

Every hire at DollarGPS shares a genuine belief that financial inequality is a problem worth solving. We're a small team by design — every person has outsized responsibility and outsized impact. We grow deliberately, not for growth's sake.

Measure Everything

We track one metric above all others: did our users' wealth improve? This keeps our product teams anchored to real-world outcomes and prevents the trap of optimising for engagement or vanity metrics that don't correlate with genuine user benefit.
