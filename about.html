Our Mission

How It Started

Born From Frustration. Built With Purpose.

DollarGPS began as a research project at one of the world's leading economics departments. Our founders — economists who had spent careers advising governments and billion-dollar institutions — kept confronting the same dispiriting reality: the tools they used every day to optimize multi-billion-dollar portfolios were completely unavailable to ordinary people.

The models existed. The Nobel Prize-winning research existed. What didn't exist was a way to translate that intellectual firepower into something an individual could actually use for their own financial life.

What started as an academic paper became a prototype. The prototype became a product. Today, DollarGPS serves thousands of individuals who now have access to the same caliber of financial intelligence that was once reserved for the ultra-wealthy.

Research Phase

Peer-reviewed study on household wealth optimization gaps published in the Journal of Finance.

University Prototype

First working model built inside a leading economics research lab, tested on 500 volunteer households.

Product Launch

DollarGPS launched publicly, bringing institutional-grade financial modeling to individual users for the first time.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Radical Transparency

We don't sell financial products. We don't earn commissions. We don't have hidden fees or undisclosed conflicts of interest. Our revenue comes only from you — which means our incentives are completely aligned with yours. Every recommendation we make is in your interest, full stop.

Academic Rigor

Every model, every algorithm, every recommendation in DollarGPS is grounded in peer-reviewed economic research — including work that has won the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Sciences. We don't guess. We don't rely on rules of thumb. We apply the most rigorous financial science available to your personal situation.

Real Impact

We measure success in actual dollars — the real, quantifiable improvement in your projected lifetime wealth. Not engagement rates. Not time-in-app. Not notification click-through. We succeed when you genuinely accumulate more wealth. That single metric keeps us honest and keeps us focused on what actually matters.
