Quick Answers

Common Questions

The answers to the questions we hear most often. More in the full FAQ.

Is DollarGPS safe to connect to my bank?

Yes. DollarGPS uses regulated Open Banking APIs — the same infrastructure used by major financial apps. We have read-only access to your transaction data and cannot initiate payments or move money under any circumstances. All data is encrypted using AES-256.

Does DollarGPS sell financial products or earn commissions?

No — never. DollarGPS earns revenue solely from your subscription. We don't sell investment products, insurance, mortgages, or any other financial products. We don't receive referral fees or commissions. Our recommendations are exclusively in your interest.

What banks does DollarGPS connect to?

DollarGPS supports over 3,000 banks and financial institutions across the UK and Ireland, with expansion to Europe and the US underway. During onboarding, you'll see a full list of supported institutions — if yours isn't listed, you can join our waitlist for when we add it.

Is DollarGPS a financial advisor?

DollarGPS provides financial education and analytical tools, not regulated financial advice. Our recommendations are based on economic models and your data, but they are informational — not advice regulated by a financial authority. For regulated advice specific to your situation, please consult a qualified financial adviser.
