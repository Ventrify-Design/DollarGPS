General
DollarGPS is a personal wealth navigation app that acts like a GPS for your finances. Just as a GPS shows you the fastest route to a destination, DollarGPS shows you the most efficient path to build your maximum possible wealth — taking into account your income, debts, spending habits, savings, investments, and life goals. It provides a complete financial roadmap with month-by-month guidance.
Most financial apps focus on tracking your spending or suggesting products to buy. DollarGPS is fundamentally different — it uses Nobel Prize-winning financial models to forecast your entire financial future and generate a personalised optimisation plan. We don't sell products, earn referral fees, or receive commissions of any kind. We exist solely to maximise your wealth using the same techniques used by the world's largest banks.

DollarGPS's Life Goals feature lets you set and track multiple goals simultaneously — retirement planning, buying a car, paying off your mortgage early, saving for a holiday, funding education, and more. The app intelligently models how all your goals interact with each other and with your wealth trajectory, showing you whether each goal is "On Track" and the projected surplus or shortfall for each one.
How It Works
After you set up your financial profile (income, debts, savings, investments, goals), DollarGPS runs sophisticated financial engineering models to calculate your current wealth trajectory. It then generates a set of "Wealth Improvements" — strategies to optimise your cashflows, debt repayment sequencing, and allocation decisions — and shows you exactly how much additional wealth you can accumulate over your lifetime by following these improvements.
No. DollarGPS is designed to optimise your wealth using your existing financial products. You don't need to switch banks, remortgage, or move any investments. The app works with what you already have, helping you make smarter decisions with your current financial structure. This is one of DollarGPS's core principles — we improve your situation without requiring you to make disruptive changes.
DollarGPS has a unique setup process that lets you experience the full power of the app in minutes — without needing to manually enter sensitive financial data. Once connected, the app immediately begins calculating your current wealth trajectory and generating personalised improvement strategies.
DollarGPS is designed for anyone who wants to take meaningful control of their financial future. Whether you're just starting out, paying off student loans, building equity in a home, planning for retirement, or managing a complex mix of assets and debts — DollarGPS provides personalised guidance for your specific situation. You don't need to be a finance expert; the app does the complex work and presents it in a clear, actionable format.
The DollarGPS model is built on Nobel Prize-winning economic research and decades of applied financial engineering — the same techniques used by the world's top-tier investment banks for institutional clients. Unlike simple budgeting calculators, our model accounts for the complex interplay of all your financial elements: debt sequencing, cashflow optimisation, compound growth effects, inflation, life events, and goal interactions. The result is a level of financial insight previously unavailable to individual consumers.
Security
Absolutely. DollarGPS uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption for all data and connects to your accounts in read-only mode — meaning we can see your data to run calculations, but we can never move or access funds. We never sell your data to third parties and operate under strict financial data protection regulations. Your privacy and security is paramount.
DollarGPS connects to your financial institutions using read-only access through regulated open banking protocols. This means we can read your transaction history and account balances to run your financial models, but we have absolutely no ability to move money, make payments, or execute any transactions on your behalf. Think of it as a view-only window into your finances.
Pricing
Never. DollarGPS does not charge commissions, accept referral fees, or earn revenue from selling or recommending financial products. We don't change anything on your long-term financial life — we simply provide financial intelligence. Our business model is subscription-based, ensuring our incentives are 100% aligned with your financial wellbeing.
DollarGPS is available as a subscription. When you consider that the average user improves their long-term wealth by $837,000+, the cost of a monthly subscription is one of the highest-return investments you can make. You can download and explore the app for free to see your projected improvements before committing.
