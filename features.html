Financial Forecasting

At the heart of DollarGPS is a financial forecasting engine unlike anything available to consumers. Rather than producing a static snapshot of your current finances, it builds a dynamic, forward-looking model of your entire financial life — projecting your wealth across multiple decades with probabilistic precision.

The model ingests your real income, spending, debts, assets, and savings rate through secure bank connections. It then applies macroeconomic variables — inflation scenarios, interest rate projections, market return distributions — to produce a range of wealth outcomes across different life paths. You see not a single number, but a probability distribution: your most likely future wealth, your optimistic scenario, and your conservative scenario.

This approach is grounded in stochastic dynamic programming — the same mathematical framework used by central banks and sovereign wealth funds. It accounts for the reality that the future is uncertain, and gives you a plan that is robust to that uncertainty rather than brittle to a single assumed outcome.

WHAT IT DOES

Projects wealth across 10, 20, and 40-year horizons with full probability distributions

Integrates live bank data — income, spending, savings, investments — updated daily

Runs Monte Carlo simulations across thousands of economic scenarios in real time

Accounts for inflation, tax, market returns, and interest rate variability

Recalculates instantly when your financial data changes

Visualises your wealth trajectory as an interactive timeline you can explore

Core Feature

Wealth Improvement Engine

The Wealth Improvement Engine is what makes DollarGPS genuinely different from every other financial app. It doesn't just show you where you are — it actively identifies specific, actionable changes that would improve your projected lifetime wealth, quantifies them in real dollars, and presents them ranked by impact.

The engine runs continuously in the background, analysing your financial profile against a comprehensive library of optimisation strategies — from debt repayment sequencing and pension contribution timing to subscription rationalisation and tax-efficient account allocation. For each potential improvement, it calculates the exact projected wealth impact over your defined time horizon.

Crucially, improvements are always expressed in real money — not percentages, not scores, not abstract categories. You see exactly which change is worth the most, so you can focus your limited financial attention where it matters most.

WHAT IT DOES

Identifies wealth improvement opportunities ranked by projected dollar impact

Analyses debt sequencing, pension contributions, and investment allocation simultaneously

Surfaces recurring subscriptions and spending patterns that reduce long-term wealth

Quantifies the compounded value of each change over your chosen time horizon

Adjusts recommendations in real time as your financial situation evolves

Never sells financial products — all recommendations are purely in your interest

Core Feature

Life Goals

Financial planning without goals is just accounting. The Life Goals feature allows you to define what you're actually working towards — a home, early retirement, a child's university education, financial independence by 50 — and integrates those targets directly into your financial forecast and improvement recommendations.

When you add a goal, DollarGPS doesn't just track your progress towards it — it recalculates your entire wealth trajectory to account for it. If achieving one goal requires trade-offs with another, the app surfaces those conflicts explicitly so you can make informed decisions rather than discover them by surprise years later.

Goals are prioritised by you, not by default rules. You can adjust the weighting between goals and see in real time how that changes your optimised financial plan. Multiple goals, competing timelines, and uncertain income are all modelled simultaneously — giving you a complete picture of your financial life, not a simplified version of it.

WHAT IT DOES

Define unlimited life goals with custom names, target dates, and amounts

Integrates goals into your wealth forecast — shows the impact of each on your trajectory

Surfaces conflicts between competing goals and models trade-off scenarios

Tracks monthly progress toward each goal with real-time probability of achievement

Adjusts your optimised savings and investment plan around goal priorities

Alerts you when life changes put a goal at risk — before it's too late to course-correct

Core Feature

Smart Budgeting

Traditional budgeting tells you what you spent. Smart Budgeting tells you what each spending decision is worth to your future self. The difference is fundamental. A traditional budget says you spent £400 on dining out last month. DollarGPS tells you that reducing that by £100 per month is worth an estimated £47,000 in additional wealth at retirement — and then shows you the twelve categories where the same logic applies most powerfully.

Smart Budgeting doesn't impose arbitrary spending limits. Instead, it surfaces the long-term cost of your current spending patterns in a way that enables genuinely informed choices. The model calculates an optimal spending allocation — a budget that maximises your lifetime wealth given your goals and income — and shows you how close or far you are from it.

Categories are automatically detected and classified from your bank transactions. No manual entry. No tedious categorisation. The engine learns your patterns and improves its classification accuracy continuously, so the longer you use DollarGPS, the more precise and personalised your budget intelligence becomes.

WHAT IT DOES

Auto-categorises all bank transactions — no manual entry required

Expresses each spending category's impact on lifetime wealth in real dollars

Generates a wealth-optimal spending allocation tailored to your income and goals

Detects irregular and one-off spending automatically — no false alarms

Machine-learning classification that improves accuracy with use over time

Compares your spending to your personal optimal — not generic benchmarks

Core Feature

Ongoing Navigation

The name DollarGPS isn't just branding — it's a philosophy. A GPS doesn't give you directions once and leave you to figure out what happens when a road is closed. It recalculates continuously, responding to the reality of your journey in real time. Ongoing Navigation is the feature that makes DollarGPS a genuine financial companion rather than a periodic report.

Every time your bank data updates — which happens automatically, daily — your entire financial model is recalculated. Your wealth forecast updates. Your improvement recommendations re-rank. Your goal probabilities shift. You always know your current financial position, your projected trajectory, and the highest-value next steps, without having to actively manage anything.

Significant life events — a new salary, a large purchase, starting a family, changing your pension — are detected automatically and trigger a full recalculation with contextual guidance. Instead of discovering months later that a life change has drifted you off course, you're immediately shown the new landscape and the best path forward from your updated position.

WHAT IT DOES

Daily automated refresh of your complete financial model — no action required

Real-time wealth trajectory updates when spending, income, or market data changes

Smart life event detection — salary changes, large purchases, new accounts

Journey view: see your entire financial path from today to your final goal

Contextual nudges when your trajectory deviates from your optimal path

Annual wealth review with year-on-year improvement breakdown in real dollars

Your Data

Built on Security

Your financial data is among the most sensitive information in your life. We treat it accordingly.

256-bit Encryption

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 — the same standard used by financial institutions worldwide. Your data is never stored unencrypted.

Read-Only Bank Access

DollarGPS connects to your bank in read-only mode via regulated Open Banking APIs. We can see your transactions — we cannot move money, make payments, or initiate any transactions.

Your Data, Your Control

We never sell your data. We never share it with third parties for advertising. You can export or delete all your data at any time. We are GDPR compliant and financially regulated.
